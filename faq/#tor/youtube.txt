YouTube is broken in Tor Browser 10.0.0 on Windows 10. Updating to TB 10.0.1
fixes the problem. Please see this ticket for more information.
https://gitlab.torproject.org/tpo/applications/tor-browser/-/issues/40140.

Some people use invidious as a proxy to YouTube https://www.invidio.us/. Others
use youtube-dl to download videos over Tor to watch locally.
